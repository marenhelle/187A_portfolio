Smartphone Application


The smartphone application is developed in collaboration with Computer Engineering students at NTNU and Sverresborg heritage museum. The purpose of the application is to make junior high-students on school trips more interested in the exhibitions and stimulate to learning.The result is a picture puzzle customized to the exhibition
'Life Pictures', where the goal is to complete as many tasks as possible. The students must visit the different parts of the exhibition and answer questions related to that area. The project dealt with the development from initial concept to finished prototype in Java. The application was developed in iterative processes with active user involvement.

Developed in 2010
